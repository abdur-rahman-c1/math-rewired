
# Problmes of Four Basic Operation

## Use of Brackets

| Priority Level  | Operation                                           |
|-----------------|-----------------------------------------------------|
| 1 (Highest)     | Brackets `( )`, `{ }`, `[ ]`                        |
| 2               | Orders (Exponents, Powers, Roots)                   |
| 3               | Division `÷` & Multiplication `×` (Left to Right)   |
| 4 (Lowest)      | Addition `+` & Subtraction `−` (Left to Right)      |
